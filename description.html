Internship Title Apply Now

Description

Want to help India learn English ? Have a passion to teach ? Here is an internship for you to make great use of your knowledge in English to make an impact and help people of our country to build their English speaking skills. What you should do

  1. Spend 4 hours everyday, between 7 am - 1 am, conducting Sling sessions - where you chat with the learners through the Sling app and help them improve their English
  2. Take 5 learners under your wing, design and execute their learning journey on Sling
  3. Evaluate the performance of your dedicated learners
  4. Provide creative solutions for teaching and progress assessment to the Sling Team
  5. Support in tutor identification and development activities in your area

(Interns need to have an Android Smartphone and access to Wifi during their work hours)

Field of Internship

Content Writing / Journalism

Skill Required

Oral Communicatoin SkillsWritten Communicatoin SkillsVocabulary

Stipend Type

Offered in range

2000 - 8000 / Month

What will you learn in this Internship ?

  1. Sling will pay an Intern Honorarium of Rs. 30 per 20-minute learning session undertaken by the intern.
  2. It is estimated that interns will earn between Rs.3000 - Rs.10,000 per month
  3. High-performing interns will be offered a freelancing option as a tutor on Sling post their internship
  4. All Sling interns will receive an internship certificate (Interns who complete a minimum of 100 sessions per month to be eligible for certification )
  5. High performing interns will also receive a personal note of appreciation from the Sling co-founders
  6. Overall - you will gain deep satisfaction of making an impact through your work
